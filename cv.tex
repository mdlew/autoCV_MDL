\subsection*{My lab pioneers imaging technologies that reveal the architecture and dynamics of life at the nanoscale.}
The Lew Lab integrates advanced optical design, computation, and physical chemistry to create multidimensional optical imaging systems that transcend conventional limits, thereby capturing molecular positions, orientations, and interactions in unprecedented detail. The team collaborates across disciplines to convert fundamental discoveries about nanoscale sensing and imaging into technologies that accelerate breakthroughs in biology, medicine, and materials science. Our innovations redefine how scientists see the invisible.
